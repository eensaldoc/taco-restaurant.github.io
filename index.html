Tacos Bronco

Tacos El Bronco specializes in authentic Mexican food in Brooklyn, NY. Come try our incredible burritos! At Tacos El Bronco, we treat our diners to the authentic dishes and cuisine that would be found in any great restaurant in Mexico. We work hard to serve our customers the traditional, delicious Mexican food in town made with fresh, high quality ingredients - in big portions too! Throw in a dose of Tex-Mex, and we have everything you could ask for if you have a Mexican food craving. Some favorites include the amazing Mole Poblano, Tacos Al Pastor, our huge Tortas, and Lengua & Tripe. Pair your meal with our flavorful green sauce or red sauce, or you can even get some mole sauce on the side. We look forward to seeing you at Tacos El Bronco, where we are proud to serve you some of the best Mexican food in Brooklyn!
